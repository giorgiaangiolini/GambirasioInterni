HISTORY

Gambirasio Interni was born in Bergamo in 1936 as a furniture store, which was soon joined by an upholstery shop. Throughout its long history, it has specialized in the collection and trade of antique carpets and chinoiserie, rare objects and one-of-a-kind pieces, getting ever closer, from the 1990s onwards, to an ad personam design of luxury residential interiors and hotels.

Since 2009 Gambirasio has been under the direction of Mimma Cervellera, for many years a reference figure of the company, who will lead the renewed Gambirasio Interni to increasingly assume the connotations of an interior tailoring atelier. For some years now he has been sharing the design part with his son Alessandro, an architect specialized in interiors and home decor.

Today Gambirasio Interni offers a complete personal interior design service for made-to-measure spaces such as tailored suits, promoting the values of its tradition in the direction of a contemporary interior architecture design.
